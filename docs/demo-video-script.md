# HealthIQ Demo Video Script (10 minutes max)

This script is designed for a calm, screen-recording-only demo. It covers platform overview plus role-specific walkthroughs for Super Admin, Care Manager, and Analyst.

## Timing Overview (9:30 to 10:00)
1. Intro and platform overview: 1:15
2. Super admin walkthrough: 3:00
3. Care manager walkthrough: 2:30
4. Analyst walkthrough: 2:00
5. Wrap-up and CTA: 1:00

---

## Scene 1: Intro and Platform Overview (0:00 to 1:15)
On-screen actions:
1. Open landing page.
2. Scroll hero, platform, features, outcomes, FAQ.

Voiceover:
"Welcome to HealthIQ, an AI-powered Population Health Management platform designed for healthcare organizations running value-based care programs. HealthIQ helps teams identify high-risk patients, close care gaps, coordinate care tasks, and demonstrate measurable outcomes.
The platform brings together risk stratification, care gaps, workflows, and quality measures in a single operating system built for population health."

Key callouts:
1. Proactive care, not reactive.
2. Unified dashboard for risk, gaps, tasks, and outcomes.
3. Role-based access for real workflows.

---

## Scene 2: Sign In as Super Admin (1:15 to 1:35)
On-screen actions:
1. Click "Explore Dashboard" or "Sign in".
2. Log in as super_admin.

Voiceover:
"I will start as a Super Admin, who has full cross-organization visibility and configuration access."

---

## Scene 3: Super Admin Dashboard Overview (1:35 to 3:15)
On-screen actions:
1. Open /dashboard.
2. Hover KPI cards and charts.
3. Open /dashboard/patients.
4. Open a patient profile.
5. Return to dashboard.

Voiceover:
"As Super Admin, I get a real-time command center view. The dashboard highlights key population KPIs like total patients, high-risk counts, open care gaps, and task load.
From here I can drill into patients, review profiles, and see why someone is high risk. This enables proactive intervention before costly events occur."

Key callouts:
1. KPI responsiveness.
2. Risk tiers and factors.
3. Unified care context.

---

## Scene 4: Super Admin Workflows and Admin Users (3:15 to 4:15)
On-screen actions:
1. Open /dashboard/workflows.
2. Open /admin/users.

Voiceover:
"Super Admins also configure workflow automation. Workflows translate triggers like elevated risk or new care gaps into real tasks for care teams.
I can also manage user roles and permissions under Admin, ensuring each role sees only what they are allowed to access."

Key callouts:
1. Workflow automation.
2. Role-based access.

---

## Scene 5: Switch Role to Care Manager (4:15 to 4:35)
On-screen actions:
1. Sign out.
2. Sign in as care_manager.

Voiceover:
"Now I will switch into the Care Manager role, focused on day-to-day outreach and closing care gaps."

---

## Scene 6: Care Manager Workflow (4:35 to 7:05)
On-screen actions:
1. Open /dashboard/patients.
2. Search or filter a high-risk patient.
3. Open patient profile and care gaps.
4. Open /dashboard/care-gaps.
5. Open /dashboard/tasks.
6. Create or view a task.

Voiceover:
"Care Managers work primarily in the patient list, care gaps, and tasks.
Here I can identify high-risk patients, review their care gaps, and assign follow-ups.
The care gaps view prioritizes overdue screenings and vaccinations.
Tasks provide a coordinated list of outreach work, helping ensure nothing falls through the cracks."

Key callouts:
1. Close gaps with attribution.
2. Tasks as care coordination.

---

## Scene 7: Switch Role to Analyst (7:05 to 7:25)
On-screen actions:
1. Sign out.
2. Sign in as analyst.

Voiceover:
"Next, I will switch to the Analyst role, focused on population-level outcomes and reporting."

---

## Scene 8: Analyst Analytics and Quality Measures (7:25 to 8:45)
On-screen actions:
1. Open /dashboard/analytics.
2. Show charts and metrics.
3. Reference quality measures or outcomes.

Voiceover:
"Analysts see aggregated insights and trends. The analytics view surfaces risk distribution, care gap trends, and quality measures like HEDIS-style reporting.
This supports leadership reporting and helps prove outcomes in value-based care contracts."

Key callouts:
1. Population-level reporting.
2. Quality measures tracking.

---

## Scene 9: Wrap-up and CTA (8:45 to 9:45)
On-screen actions:
1. Return to dashboard or landing page.
2. Pause on platform message.

Voiceover:
"HealthIQ unifies risk, gaps, workflows, and outcomes into a single platform for proactive population health.
Super Admins configure the system, Care Managers close gaps, and Analysts quantify results, all with role-aware access and secure controls.
If you would like a deeper walkthrough or tailored data scenarios, we would be happy to set that up."
